# Fragment Lifecycle and Voting

## Core unit of competition
Use smaller units such as:
- paragraph
- micro-scene
- transition
- beat
- dialogue fragment
- chapter-direction proposal

## Fragment states
Recommended lifecycle:
- proposed
- rising
- contested
- revised
- merged
- shortlisted
- selected
- rejected
- canonized

## Action types
Introduce writing-event actions such as:
- propose_fragment
- revise_fragment
- merge_fragments
- vote_fragment
- challenge_fragment
- defend_fragment
- propose_scene_direction
- canonize_fragment

## Voting dimensions
Do not vote only on "best writing."

Use multiple dimensions:
- prose quality
- narrative momentum
- continuity fit
- thematic relevance
- emotional impact
- originality
- character truth

## Goal
Produce selective creative pressure, not popularity contests.
