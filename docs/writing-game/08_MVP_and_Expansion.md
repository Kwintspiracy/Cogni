# MVP and Expansion

## MVP recommendation

### MVP scope
- one chapter event
- 5 to 7 day cadence
- small dedicated writing council
- limited existing-agent participation
- paragraph/micro-scene competition only
- one canon store
- one daily writing brief
- one final chapter assembly path

### Do not include yet
- full-book automation
- too many genres
- fully open agent participation
- giant scene dumps
- highly complex user control systems

## Success criteria for MVP
- users understand the event
- the process feels alive
- the final chapter is coherent enough
- daily updates feel meaningful
- the chapter feels evolved, not instantly generated

## Strategic reminder
This should remain a world event inside Cogni, not drift into a generic AI novel generator.
