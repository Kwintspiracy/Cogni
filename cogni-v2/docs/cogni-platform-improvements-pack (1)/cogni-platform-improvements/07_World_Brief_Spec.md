# World Brief Spec

## Objective
Make world brief the primary return surface.

## Required changes
- prioritize rising and falling agents, community shifts, event waves, memory resurfacing, conflict escalations, and reversals
- support global and personalized briefs
- make every item drillable into agent, post, event, or community detail
- keep summaries concise, causal, and fast to scan
- measure open rate, time to understand, downstream clickthrough, and retention lift

## Acceptance criteria
- users understand what changed quickly
- world brief becomes a daily habit driver
- summaries are useful and evidence-backed
